Yi Yang

  My name is Yi Yang(杨益), a sophomore at the University of Science and Technology of China. I major in Statistics and minor in Communications at the School of the Gifted Young. I am passionate about bridging the gap between Statistics, AI and Social Science, as I believe that these fields can work in tandem to solve complex real-world problems.

Currently, I am interested in exploring topics related to scalable GNNs, generative models, as well as the associated privacy and security concerns.
